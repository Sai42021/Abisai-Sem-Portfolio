About Me

Hello, my name is Abisai, but you can call me Sai

That's a nickname I gave myself when I first moved to Windhoek to study computer science at the Namibia University of Science and Technology.

As a "newbie" to city life, it was really hard to adjust to my new surroundings at first. I quickly made new friends and pushed hard with my academics, although it certainly wasn't and still isn't easy.

My second year at NUST was when I first got introduced to web development via simple HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I fell in love with the process of writing scripts and code to display elegant and transformative designs onto a web page or into mobile apps. I've honed in on design and development ever since, and it's a raging passion for me to this day.

When I'm not winning hackathons or burying my nose in books, I love watching films and catching up on some books by my favorite author Robert Greene. I find the 48 Laws of Power particularly enlightening.
