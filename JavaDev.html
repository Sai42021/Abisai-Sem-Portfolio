Vending machine programme

Description

Vending machine programme. Prompts user to enter code depending on whether they are patrons or admins. Patron/user enters quantity of each product they would like to purchase. The total for all items is calculated and user enters amount of cash they would like to insert into the vending machine. The change is then calculated and a receipt produced.

Codes

Item code: 3692
Owners pin: 4812
Verification code: 8162

What I learned

  • Planning in forms of Pseudocode and Flowcharts
  • Receipt information calculations and formatting
  • Change calculations and print out
  • Management options by Admin
  • Customer options and interactions
